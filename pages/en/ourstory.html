Our Story

Our story began in the Fall of 2015 in Washington, D.C. We were pursuing our Master’s degrees, while pursuing love on Bumble. Two faithful swipes led to a match and the start…of non-stop texting. We mildly neglected our studies over the next couple of days talking and getting to know each other, when we realized, ‘hey, maybe there is something here’!

We decided to take our conversation out of the digital world and into the real one by meeting up for coffee at a place called Tryst, which, in addition to being a cute coffee shop in Adams Morgan, means ‘a private romantic rendezvous between lovers’. If this was lost on us in the moment, it most certainly is not lost on us now. Our easy digital repertoire instantly translated in person. Sparks flew, laughter rang, and our worries melted away. That is until we remembered that our higher education still called – we both had assignments due the next day.

Not wanting our date to end but recognizing the importance of our studies, we did what any reasonable, studious millennials would do: transitioned our coffee date into an impromptu study date. The rest, as many would say, is history.

After 5 years together filled with twists and turns, we are closing one chapter in our lives together to start the next. We can’t wait to celebrate it with you all!
