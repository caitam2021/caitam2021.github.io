Covid-19

Dear family and friends,
Unfortunately, the COVID-19 pandemic is still ongoing and therefore, for the safety of our guests, we will be taking certain measures to reduce the risk of transmission as much as possible.

Prior to the Wedding
We are asking all people (independent of vaccination status) who are attending our wedding in person to take a COVID-19 test no earlier than Tuesday, November 23rd , and to confirm your test results by updating your RSVP.

If taking a COVID-19 test is not an option for your please let us know. This deeply saddens us because we love you and will miss you, but as much as we want to start our marriage with you present, we need to consider the safety of our family and friends.

During the Wedding
We will provide masks and hand sanitizers at the wedding and encourage you to make use of them as much as possible.

Following the Wedding
If you develop symptoms after the wedding please get tested ASAP and let us know of any positive test results, so that we may alert other guests to also get tested. Our goal with these measures is to keep everyone safe and to give you and the rest of our guests some peace of mind so that we can celebrate big! Thank you for your patience and understanding, and we cannot wait to be together with you in person again. Please reach out to us if you have any additional questions.

Love,
Andrea & John
